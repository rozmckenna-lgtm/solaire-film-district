Biography / Biographie

Rosalia McKenna

Founder & Producer

EN Founder & Producer of Silver Road Media (France). Former Founder & Managing Director of Silver Road Studios (London), serving BBC and Disney. MA in Television & Film Set Production.

FR Fondatrice & productrice de Silver Road Media (France). Ancienne fondatrice & directrice generale de Silver Road Studios (Londres), au service de la BBC et Disney. MA en production de decors pour la television et le cinema.

EN

Developing Solaire Film District

Rosalia McKenna is developing the Solaire Film District as a sustainable production campus in Occitanie, bringing together her extensive experience in audiovisual production infrastructure with a commitment to environmental and regional responsibility.

With a career spanning international production for major broadcasters including BBC and Disney, she brings proven expertise in building and managing studio facilities to this ambitious new project in southern France.

FR

Developper le Solaire Film District

Rosalia McKenna developpe le Solaire Film District en tant que campus de production durable en Occitanie, combinant sa vaste experience en infrastructure de production audiovisuelle avec un engagement envers la responsabilite environnementale et territoriale.

Forte d'une carriere en production internationale pour de grands diffuseurs tels que la BBC et Disney, elle apporte une expertise reconnue dans la creation et la gestion d'infrastructures de studios a ce nouveau projet ambitieux dans le sud de la France.
